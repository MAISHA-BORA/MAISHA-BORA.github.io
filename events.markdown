# Upcoming Events & Programs

Stay connected with Maisha Bora Youth Foundation and join us in making a difference in the lives of teenagers in Karatu District.

---

## Weekly Programs

### Spiritual Growth Workshops
**Every Saturday | 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM**  
Mang'ola Community Center

Weekly sessions focusing on values clarification, mindfulness, and personal development. Guest speakers from various faiths and philosophies provide diverse perspectives on living purposeful lives.

**Open to:** Teenagers ages 13-20  
**Registration:** Walk-in or contact us in advance

---

### Social Skills Development Workshop
**Every Wednesday | 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM**  
Youth Center, Karatu District

Interactive workshops covering:
- Communication skills
- Conflict resolution
- Teamwork and collaboration
- Leadership development

**Open to:** Teenagers ages 13-20  
**Registration Required:** Contact us at info@maishabora.org

## How to Participate

### For Teenagers:
1. **Register:** Contact us via phone, email, or visit our office
2. **Assessment:** Brief meeting to understand your needs and goals
3. **Enrollment:** Join programs that match your interests and needs
4. **Participate:** Attend regularly and engage fully
5. **Grow:** Develop skills, build relationships, and plan your future

### For Parents/Guardians:
We encourage family involvement! Contact us to:
- Learn about our programs
- Discuss your teenager's needs
- Understand how we can help
- Get involved in supporting our mission
